What is SpaceX ?

Space Exploration Technologies Corp. (SpaceX) is an American aerospace manufacturer and space transportation services company headquartered in Hawthorne, California. SpaceX was founded in 2002 by Elon Musk

What is our goal ?

Our goal is to reduce space transportation costs to enable the colonization of Mars.

What are our spaceships?

SpaceX manufactures the Falcon 9 and Falcon Heavy launch vehicles, several rocket engines, Dragon cargo, crew spacecraft and Starlink communications satellites and spaceship for your family.
