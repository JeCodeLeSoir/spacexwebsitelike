Capsule Crew Dragon

Crew Dragon is equipped with an integrated launch escape system (LES) consisting of eight SuperDraco engines, capable of accelerating the vehicle away from the rocket in an emergency. The spacecraft features redesigned solar arrays and a modified outer mold line compared to the original Dragon, and possesses new flight computers and avionics. As of March 2020, four operational Dragon 2 spacecraft have been manufactured, as well as several unflown test articles. Crew Dragon serves as one of two spacecraft that are expected to transport crews to and from the ISS under NASA's Commercial Crew Program, the other being the Boeing CST-100 Starliner;
